<br />
<br />

<p>
  Withdrawing from Stoqey is very easy, you simply sell out your shares, you
  create a payment method, then withdraw! please follow this guide step by step!
</p>
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />

### 1. Sell shares funds

<p>
  From the main menu, please go to **Trade** page, (like in the image below),
  click trade button or "Close" from portfolio list enter the number of shares
  that you want to sell from your account, then submit!
</p>
<br />

![Sell shares](/blog/withdraw-sell.png)

<br />
<br />
<br />

### 2. Add payment method from Withdraw Screen

<p>
  from the main menu select **Funds** page, then navigate to "Withdraw" sub-menu
  like in the image below then click on the "Add payment method" button
</p>
<br />

![Add payment method](/blog/withdraw-method.png)

<br />
<br />
<br />

### 3. Select payment method

<p>After adding the payment method, click on it like in the image below!</p>
<br />

![Select payment method](/blog/withdraw-method-select.png)

<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />

### \*. Enter the amount to withdraw

<p>
  Now enter the amount you want to withdraw and click on "Submit withdraw
  request"
</p>
<br />

![Add payment method](/blog/withdraw-01.png)

<br />
<br />
<br />

<p>
  PS: To avoid your SELL orders from being stuck/idle from the order book,
  please submit your order with a slightly lower price than the current market
  price (orders in "ASK" section), let's say if 1 share is at $102 like in the first
  image above, but the top order in "ASK" sections is $99, so we submit our order
  at $98 so that the system prioritizes your order first!
</p>
